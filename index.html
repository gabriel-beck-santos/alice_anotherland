History

Alice was lost again, but this time in a whole new universe. She is trapped in the Disney universe and must find her way back to Wonderland and needs your help!

Journey

On your journey you will find characters from other universes that will help you achieve your goal. Each has its own story, and will help you stay calm and move on!

End

In the end what matters is the people you met and stories that helped build who you are. Even returning to the same place, with the same friends, memories and experiences are eternal!
